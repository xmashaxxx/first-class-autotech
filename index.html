Why C&C?

More Than Your Regular Repair Shop

Most shops fix engines. Some handle electrics. Others cut keys. At C & C Parts & Repair Inc, we do all of it — under one roof. Engine to computer, brakes to ECU, A/C to brand new keys: we cover every aspect of your vehicle.

Precision Diagnostics

Diagnostics for
Every System

Modern vehicles are computers on wheels. At C & C Parts & Repair Inc, we use factory-level diagnostic tools to read every module, every system, every fault — on any make or model.

No guessing. No parts-swapping. We find the exact issue, explain it clearly, and fix it right the first time.

  • Full OBD scan — engine, transmission, ABS, airbag
  • Live data analysis & freeze frame reading
  • ECU fault diagnosis & module programming
  • All warning lights — identified and resolved
  • Works on domestic, import & European
